Wisconsin Petition Drives

Signature Maps

Interactive maps of petition signatures across Wisconsin for three campaigns — Sara Rodriguez, Francesca Hong, and Mandela Barnes — by city and by county.

Single-campaign maps

Sara Rodriguez — Signatures by City
Every Wisconsin city where Rodriguez collected signatures, with each circle sized by the number of signatures. About 3,200 signatures across 280+ cities.

Comparison maps

Who Leads Where
Each city colored by whichever campaign drew the largest share of its own signatures there. Tabs toggle between the combined view and each individual campaign.
who_leads_where_map.htmlOpen map
Over / Under-Index
For a selected campaign, each city is colored by whether the campaign drew more (red) or less (blue) than its proportional share relative to the three campaigns combined. Tabs switch between campaigns.
